Why is Apex the best for admissions advice?

Apex offers flexible pricing tailored to each student’s specific needs. Below, you can explore both custom options and their associated pricing directly on this page. Our Standard Plan is all-inclusive and provides the most comprehensive level of support, offering the strongest opportunity for success by including every service we offer. Does Apex guide international students?

Yes. We support students from around the world with admissions strategies, application guidance, and international credential evaluation.

Does Apex coach essay writing?

Yes. Our mentors help students craft compelling personal narratives, brainstorm strong topics, and refine essays for clarity, voice, and impact.

Does Apex guide passion projects?

Yes. We help students design and execute meaningful passion projects that highlight their strengths and interests.

Does Apex help students find internships?

Yes. We advise students on finding and applying for internships, including strategic positioning and follow-up.

Does Apex help students find research opportunities?

Yes. We guide students to research programs, faculty outreach, and project planning that strengthen their academic profile.

Does Apex guarantee admissions to any universities?

No. We provide high-quality guidance and support, but admission decisions are made by universities.

Does Apex help students pick class schedules?

Yes. We work with students to choose coursework that aligns with their college goals and academic strengths.

Does Apex guide students on choosing their career pathways?

Yes. We help students clarify career goals early so their college list and major choices support long-term aspirations.

Does Apex help students prepare for standardized testing?

Yes, we help students prepare for the SAT and ACT. We provide access to tutoring and strategic planning to ensure students acquire sufficiently high scores to support their applications.

Does Apex help students determine which universities to apply to?

Yes. Choosing the right mix of universities is essential. We help students build a balanced school list that fits their academic profile and campus culture preferences.

Does Apex have any hidden fees?

No! All fees are transparent and discussed up-front. We believe in building trust with families, which starts with clear and honest pricing.
